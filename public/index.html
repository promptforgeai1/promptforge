How PromptForge Works
Everything you need to know to get the most out of PromptForge — from your first generation to saving your best outputs.
⚡ Quick start — 3 steps
1
Pick a generator from the sidebar on the left
8 generators available — Surprise Me, Make Money, Short-Form Video, Sales & Marketing, 3D Doll, Viral Story, Smart Image, Design Studio. If you are new — start with Surprise Me.
2
Fill in the form — or pick from the idea suggestions
No idea what to write? Each generator has a 💡 idea picker — a dropdown with ready-made specific ideas you can choose from. Pick one and it fills in the box for you. Or type your own if you already know what you want. You can also choose 1, 2, or 3 variations before generating.
3
Copy your output and use it — or save it for later
Every output is ready to use immediately. Click ⎘ Copy to copy everything. Click ♡ Save to save the output to your library so you can come back to it later.
🔢
Variations — generate 1, 2, or 3 at once
Before clicking generate, choose how many variations you want. Picking 3 gives you 3 completely different outputs in one go — all generated at the same time. Each one has its own Copy button so you can pick the one you prefer.
📱
Repurpose for All Platforms — one idea, four formats
After generating a Surprise Me, Short-Form Video, or Viral Story output — click 📱 Repurpose for All Platforms. The AI takes your content and reformats it as a TikTok script, a Twitter/X thread, a LinkedIn post, and a Newsletter intro — all in one click. Each version is natively formatted for that platform.
⎘
Copy individual sections — not just everything at once
Hover over any section in your output — the Hook, the Script, the Caption, the Quick Prompt — and a small ⎘ Copy button appears. Click it to copy just that section. Useful when you only need one part of the output.
🙈
No Face Content — create without ever going on camera
In the Short-Form Video generator, select a No Face style — text on screen with voiceover, screen recording, AI avatar narration, or carousel/slideshow. The AI produces scripts and content specifically designed for formats that never require you to film yourself.
📸
Photo upload — 3D Doll and Smart Image
On the 3D Doll generator you can upload a photo of yourself or anyone. The AI analyses your skin tone, hair type, and features and builds the prompt around you — so the 3D doll looks like you. On Smart Image you can upload any reference image and the AI creates a prompt based on what it sees.
♡
Save to your library
Every output has a ♡ Save button. Click it to save the output to your personal library. You can access your saved outputs any time from Saved Prompts in the sidebar — even if you close the app and come back days later. Good for saving hooks, chibi prompts, or scripts you want to use later.

📱 What to do with your output — complete beginner guide
🎬 For Video Content
HOOK = your very first words when you press record. Say it immediately. No introduction.
SCRIPT = everything you say after the hook. Film yourself speaking it naturally.
CAPTION = the text you type when uploading to TikTok or Instagram. Copy and paste it there.
HASHTAGS = paste these at the bottom of your caption when uploading.
🖼️ For Image Prompts
QUICK PROMPT = copy this and paste into ChatGPT, Adobe Firefly, or Canva AI to generate your image.
IMAGE PROMPT in Surprise Me = use this separately to create a thumbnail or image post alongside your video.
Best tool for beginners = ChatGPT. Open chatgpt.com → click the image icon → paste → generate.
💡 The image prompt in your output is NOT for your video. It is a separate tool. Use it to create a picture that you post alongside your video or separately on its own. Your video is filmed by YOU speaking the hook and script.
🎯 How to get non-generic hooks and outputs
The specificity rule — the most important thing to know
The more specific your input, the more specific and powerful your output. PromptForge is trained to produce non-generic content — but it needs specific material to work with. A vague input produces a decent output. A specific input produces a wow output.
✗ Weak inputs
"fitness journey"
"my business"
"social media tips"
"anyone" as audience
✓ Strong inputs
"trained 90 days, lost nothing, then this"
"made £0 for 8 months as a creator"
"I stopped posting and got more followers"
"busy Black mums who want natural hair"
If you are not happy with an output — click Improve This Idea below the result. This tells the AI the first version was not sharp enough and forces a stronger, more specific second attempt. You can also make your input more specific and generate again.
